Raspberry Pi:

Welcome to a series on the Raspberry Pi. The Raspberry Pi is a fully-fledged mini computer, capable of doing whatever you might do with a computer. It comes with 4x USB, HDMI, LAN, built-in Bluetooth/WiFi support, 1GB RAM, 1.2GHz quad-core ARM CPU, 40 GPIO (General Purpose Input Output) pins, audio and composite video output, and more. Rather than not having many choices, instead, your options are staggeringly large!

This tutorial series will introduce you to the Raspberry Pi and some of the things you can do with it!

Parts list: https://pythonprogramming.net/introduction-raspberry-pi-tutorials
